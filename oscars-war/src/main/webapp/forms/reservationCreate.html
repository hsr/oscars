Required inputs are bordered in green. The source and destination can be URN topology identifers or a registered friendly name. Click on the boxes associated with the start and end dates to bring up a calendar widget. The reservation time slot defaults to now, and now + 15 minutes, respectively, if you leave the dates and times empty.

WARNING: Entering a series of hops in the Path field may alter routing behavior for the selected flow. Hops can be URN topology identifiers or registered friendly names. Note that the path field will expand to the number of lines occurring in the hops list.

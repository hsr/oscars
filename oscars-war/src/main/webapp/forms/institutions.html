Use this form to manage the institution table. Clicking on ADD will clear the EDIT text field. Add the row by adding text and clicking on SAVE. Clicking on a row will bring up its name in the EDIT text field and take you out of ADD mode. To then modify that name, edit the text and click on the SAVE button. To delete that row, click on the DELETE button. This will not succeed unless there are no users belonging to that institution.
